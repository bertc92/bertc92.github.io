About Me

I'm an aspiring game designer with project experience in Unity, Unity VR, Ren'py, and tabletop mediums. I'm also proficient in programming languages like C#, C++, Python, and other OOP languages. I've made and collaborated on design solutions for group projects over the years. I've also done both web and database design, both frontend and backend including PHP, mySQL, HTML, Javascript and CSS. I'm currently graduating with a Bachelor's in SFU's Interactive & Arts Technology. I also know how to work with Microsoft Office and the Adobe software suites. I have a career interest in games and/or film. I've always been drawn to the design process of making games. On the film side of things, I love camerawork, photography, and a well-written story. I've filmed and edited a microdocumentary as well as a short film in my studies. Writing and reading are interests for me, and I would love the opportunity to write and/or design for a narrative-driven game.

Some of my favourite films include Taxi Driver, Apocalypse Now, and Mad Max Fury Road. My favourite games include Fallout 1 & 2, Planescape Torment, and Vampire the Masquerade: Bloodlines.
